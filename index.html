Who We Are

The Denver Miniatures Alliance is a community of hobbyists and wargamers based in Denver, Colorado. We gather to paint miniatures, build armies, and push toy soldiers across tabletops together.

Whether you're a seasoned veteran with hundreds of painted models or looking to get into the hobby, you'll find a welcoming home here. We play a wide range of games — from Frostgrave, Rangers of Shadow Deep, A Billion Suns, Xenos Rampant, and everything in between.

We organize regular game nights across the Denver metro area. The best way to find out what's happening is to join our Discord server below.
